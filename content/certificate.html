Detail of Certification MTCNA

MikroTik Certified Network Associate (MTCNA) is a certification issued by MikroTik validating that an individual possesses fundamental knowledge and skills in managing network devices using MikroTik hardware and software (RouterOS).


Below is a detailed breakdown of the MTCNA certification, including the topics and skills covered:

  1. Introduction to MikroTik RouterOS
  2. DHCP Configuration
  3. Bridging Concepts & Config
  4. Static & Dynamic Routing
  5. Wireless Networking
  6. Firewall Filters & NAT
  7. Quality of Service (QoS)
  8. Tunneling Protocols

For more details regarding the MTCNA curriculum, you can visit the official outline here.

Detail of Certification MTCTCE

MikroTik Certified Traffic Control Engineer (MTCTCE) is an advanced-level certification issued by MikroTik. This certification emphasizes the deep knowledge and technical skills required to manage network traffic efficiently using specialized MikroTik RouterOS tools.

Below is a detailed breakdown of the MTCTCE certification, including the advanced networking skills covered:

  1. Packet Flow Diagram (Deep Dive)
  2. Firewall Filters, Mangle, and NAT Chain
  3. Quality of Service (QoS / Queues)
  4. DNS Client & Cache Optimization
  5. DHCP Client, Relay, and Server Management
  6. Web Proxy & Advanced Caching
