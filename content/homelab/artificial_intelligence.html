AI for Security Operations

This section illustrates the roadmap for integrating Large Language Models and Agentic AI into the modern security infrastructure.

Phase 1: Research

Agentic AI Frameworks

Exploring multi-agent orchestration using frameworks like LangChain and CrewAI to automate routine incident response playbooks and intelligence gathering.

Phase 2: Experiment

LLM Security & Red-Teaming

Simulating prompt injection attacks and data leakage scenarios to build robust guardrails for enterprise AI applications and secure model deployment.

Phase 3: Development

AI-Powered Threat Hunting

Utilizing machine learning models for anomaly detection within VPC flow logs and SIEM data to identify zero-day patterns and stealthy lateral movements.

Phase 4: Future

Self-Healing Infrastructure

Integrating AI decision-makers with Ansible and Terraform to automatically patch vulnerabilities and remediate misconfigurations in real-time.
