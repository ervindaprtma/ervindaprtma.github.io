About Me

I am a Network Engineer with 5+ years of experience in Enterprise Networking and Network Security, specializing in routing, WAN architecture, and security-driven infrastructure design.

Having completed the CCNP Enterprise (ENCOR & ENARSI) learning track, I strengthened my expertise in advanced routing (BGP, OSPF, EIGRP), MPLS, VPN technologies, and enterprise network optimization.

My career began in the ISP sector, where I was responsible for designing and optimizing customer distribution networks. This foundation shaped my capability in large-scale operations, infrastructure monitoring, performance tuning, and security implementation.

Currently, I focus on building security-centric and visibility-driven network architectures, supported by hands-on experience with FortiGate and MikroTik firewalls, as well as advanced web security testing through CWSE and CAPT certifications.


My current areas of focus include:
  • Log & traffic flow analysis (FortiGate, DNS, ElastiFlow) to transform telemetry into actionable security and performance insights
  • Firewall policy engineering & optimization (FortiGate, MikroTik)
  • Enterprise WAN architecture (IPsec, SD-WAN, HA design)
  • Advanced routing implementation (BGP, OSPF, EIGRP, Route Redistribution)
  • Web application security testing (OWASP-based vulnerability assessment)
  • Infrastructure monitoring & proactive troubleshooting
  • Continuous threat awareness aligned with modern attack vectors

My Mission

To design and strengthen resilient, secure, and performance-optimized enterprise infrastructures by combining advanced routing expertise, security engineering principles, and offensive security awareness — enabling organizations to proactively mitigate risks and stay adaptive in an evolving cyber threat landscape.
